Explore The Chianti Region in Tuscany, Italy

Look at this website to start your journey into exploring the Chianti Region

If you are planning a trip to Italy, try the Chianti Region. This beautiful area is rich with history and culture of the old world. Chianti's architecture is mainly from the mediaeval period. These beautiful buildings make this region so beautiful. The Chianti region has wonderful architecture, idyllic scenery, and delicious chianti wine. The region is famous for its wine, however there are many other beautiful qualities.
